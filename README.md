#Project: Space Counter Energy (SCE) Theory

An alternative physical framework to unify General Relativity and Quantum Mechanics while replacing Dark Matter and Dark Energy hypotheses.

##🌌 Core Vision
This repository serves as the primary archive for the research, mathematical modeling, and logical derivation of the Space Counter Energy (SCE) theory.

The core objectives of this research are:

To refute the hypothetical existence of "Dark Matter" and "Dark Energy" within current cosmological models.

To propose the SCE field as the fundamental mechanism driving cosmic expansion and galactic dynamics.

To explore a logical unification of General Relativity and Quantum Field Theory through the SCE framework.

##🧩 Core Hypotheses

1. Replacing Dark Energy & Dark Matter
This theory posits that observed galactic rotation anomalies and the accelerating expansion of the universe are not driven by undiscovered substances or energies. Instead, they result from the Space Counter Energy (SCE) field—an intrinsic property of space itself.

2. Mechanism of the SCE Field
Concept: Space is not a vacuum backdrop but a dynamic field with inherent "negative pressure" characteristics.

Function: On a macroscopic scale, the SCE field acts as a "phase cancellation" of gravity, maintaining the structural integrity of galaxies and driving cosmic expansion without the need for additional mass terms.

3. Toward a Unified Field Theory
By introducing a quantized fluctuation model of the SCE field, this research seeks the convergence point between the geometric properties of General Relativity and the dynamical nature of Quantum Physics.
